About

Since our founding, Marcus Dispatch Services LLC has been known for quality services, exceptional efficiency and the highest level of professionalism. No matter what service you’re looking for, we guarantee to not only meet, but exceed your expectations and ensure your full satisfaction.

We strive to demonstrate solid understanding of logistics and transportation with ability to foresee potential service issues. Provide shipment level support to carrier sales team.

Work with team manager to understand all customer information and needs to accurately provide the best service for each order accepted by the team. Understand all functionality within our company’s system to provide accurate updates to team and/or customers. Work closely with broker under assigned orders to accurately insure each load is being picked up and delivered on time as scheduled.

Insure service meets customer requirements by being proactive on pick-up and delivery of loads. Enter correct data in the system so each team member can accurately perform daily responsibilities. Work with team manager and broker to accurately provide updates under each load.

Work with customers with accessorial charges that occur while providing accurate information to team manager and broker. Address any questions/issues that occur during time of pick-up through time of delivery. Work with each customer assigned to the team to provide valuable and superior service. Help team manager maintain a strong relationship with each carrier Track/trace each order assigned to the team to provide accurate updates to the team and customers.

Maintain accurate billing information for assigned customers within the team to provide the billing department all rate confirmations. Understand all customer website portals to accurately work with assigned freight for each order and trace all assigned loads.
